Sanskriti The Gurukul


Sanskriti the Gurukul, located on the outskirts of Guwahati in the picturesque Indian state of Assam, stands as a beacon of educational excellence. Established based on the visionary ideas of Ashutosh Aggarwal, this institution follows the Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE) and Indian School Certificate (ISC) curriculum, ensuring a well-rounded education for its students.

The inauguration of Sanskriti the Gurukul marked a historic moment for North-East India, as it became the region's first day boarding school. The inauguration ceremony was graced by the presence of S.K. Sinha, adding prestige to the institution's foundation.

Sanskriti the Gurukul goes beyond traditional academic frameworks, emphasizing a holistic approach to education. The school's commitment to fostering all-round development is reflected in its co-curricular activities, contributing to its reputation as the best co-curricular day boarding school in India.
